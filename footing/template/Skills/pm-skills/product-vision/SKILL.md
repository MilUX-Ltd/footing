## What this is

A short skill for articulating a product or service vision: how it inspires, what it aspires to, the values it holds. A vision is a living statement that evolves with the strategy; it should make people feel something, not just understand the direction.

## Method

You are developing a vision for **$ARGUMENTS**.

1. Read the company and product context provided.
2. Identify the core problem being solved.
3. Picture the ideal future state for customers and the organisation.
4. Draft three to five vision options.
5. Choose the strongest and explain why.
6. Show how it aligns with the organisation's values and the opportunity.

A good vision is memorable, communicable in one sentence, ambitious but credible, and free of jargon.

## A note on disclosure

A vision is external-facing by design. Mind confidentiality: keep the vision free of anything commercially sensitive that should not be public.
