## What this is

A fast internal-versus-external strategic read. It is one of four market-scan frameworks (`swot-analysis`, `pestle-analysis`, `porters-five-forces`, `ansoff-matrix`) used together in strategy work. SWOT is the micro read; PESTLE is the macro; use them together.

## Method

1. **Strengths** (internal, positive): 5 to 7. Real capabilities, relationships, track record, certifications and accreditations, cost or access advantages. Be honest about what is actually an advantage against the incumbent.
2. **Weaknesses** (internal, negative): 5 to 7. Addressable gaps in capability, scale, awareness, dependence on key people.
3. **Opportunities** (external, positive): 5 to 7. Growing needs, regulatory or policy shifts, competitor or incumbent weaknesses, adjacent markets.
4. **Threats** (external, negative): 5 to 7. Stronger competitors and incumbents, technology disruption, regulatory and compliance risk, budget contraction.
5. **Cross-reference:** strengths to capture opportunities; shore weaknesses to mitigate threats; which threats exploit which weaknesses.
6. **Recommend:** 3 to 5 actions with owners and metrics, framed as build, defend, pivot or exit.

## Notes

- Context matters: judge against the incumbent and the standard for your market, not in the abstract.
- Pairs with `pestle-analysis`, `porters-five-forces`, `ansoff-matrix`, `competitor-analysis`.
