## What this is

A 2x2 of growth options across products and markets, ordered by risk. Use it for your own growth choices or a customer's.

|  | Current market | New market |
|---|---|---|
| **Current product** | Market penetration | Market development |
| **New product** | Product development | Diversification |

## The four quadrants

1. **Market penetration** (current product, current market), win more of the existing account or customer base, displace the incumbent, reduce churn, deepen use. Lowest risk.
2. **Market development** (current product, new market), a new customer segment for the same offering: a new region, a new industry vertical, a new customer size band, or a new channel. Medium risk; mind any regulatory or accreditation gates specific to the new market.
3. **Product development** (new product, current market), an adjacent capability for the same customer. Medium risk.
4. **Diversification** (new product, new market), a new capability for a new customer. Highest risk; usually through partnership, acquisition or a deliberate new venture.

## Method

Define the current product and market; for each quadrant identify 2 to 3 specific opportunities with size, resources and competitive dynamics; prioritise by strategic fit, potential, feasibility and defensibility; build a phased roadmap for the top two or three with risks and mitigations. Render the matrix.

## Notes

- Win one quadrant before spreading across all four.
