## What this is

The macro read in a market-scan set, complementary to SWOT's micro read. It is the disciplined way to map the operating environment before committing to a strategy or a market.

## The six factors

1. **Political**, government policy and regulation relevant to the sector, spending priorities and public funding where applicable, election and policy-cycle risk, trade and international relations affecting the business.
2. **Economic**, the client organisation's budget cycle where selling to larger customers, interest rates and financing costs, inflation against fixed-price contracts, exchange rates on imported goods or services.
3. **Social**, recruitment and skills availability, customer and public attitudes and expectations, demographic shifts affecting demand.
4. **Technological**, the pace of technology adoption in the sector, the gap between a promising pilot and being awarded ongoing budget, cyber and data-handling demands, automation and AI affecting the business model.
5. **Legal**, relevant regulation and licensing, data protection, procurement or contract law and the route to contract, IP and liability. Some sectors gate market access behind accreditation, certification or clearance; note where that applies.
6. **Environmental**, sustainability requirements and expectations, resource and supply-chain resilience, climate-driven demand or risk.

## Method

Identify 3 to 5 factors per category; rate impact and likelihood (high, medium, low); prioritise by impact times likelihood; develop responses (leverage, mitigate, comply); name leading indicators to monitor and assumptions to research. Render the result.
