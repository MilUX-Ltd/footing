## Why this outcome matters to the customer

Refined through scoping conversations. Capture:

- The operational, commercial, or organisational pain the customer is responding to.
- The decision or change the customer wants to enable.
- The measure or signal that would tell them the outcome has been achieved.

## What success looks like

One or two paragraphs describing the world the customer wants to be in once this engagement has delivered. Concrete enough that scope can be tested against it ("does this deliverable move us toward the success state?").
