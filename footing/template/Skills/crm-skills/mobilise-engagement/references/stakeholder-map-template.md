## How to read this map

Four quadrants by influence (power to impact the engagement) and interest (how much they care about the outcome). Each stakeholder lands in one. Engagement strategy follows from the quadrant:

- **High influence, high interest.** Close collaboration. Decision-making, regular updates, deep involvement.
- **High influence, low interest.** Keep satisfied. Address concerns, periodic updates, no over-communication.
- **Low influence, high interest.** Keep informed and involved. Maintain support, regular updates.
- **Low influence, low interest.** Monitor. Minimal communication.
