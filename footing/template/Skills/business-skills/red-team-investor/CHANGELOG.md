# red-team-investor changelog

## 1.0.0, 2026-06-25

Initial release.

- Critiques any investor-facing content (pitch deck, one-pager, business plan, financial model, cold email, investment memo, teaser, data room) from a sceptical investor's point of view.
- Organised around the market / product / execution risk triad; every weakness maps to a named risk.
- Calibrates the bar to stage (pre-seed, seed, Series A) and to content type, works the standard lenses (team, problem, market, product, traction, unit economics, GTM, moat, financials, ask, terms, risk), sweeps for the common red flags, adds sector nuance (deep tech and TRL, regulated, marketplace, SaaS, consumer), and lists the hard diligence questions the content is least ready for.
- Reviews only; it does not rewrite the pitch or contact investors. Benchmarks cite public investor and accelerator sources and are dated 2024 to 2026 as bands, not fixed thresholds.
