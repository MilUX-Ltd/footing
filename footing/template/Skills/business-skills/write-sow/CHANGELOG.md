# write-sow changelog

## 1.0.0, 2026-06-19

Initial release.

- Turns a transcript, job spec, statement of requirement, or bullet-point idea into an outcome-based Statement of Work.
- Shapes the contract to support an outside-IR35 position (control, substitution, mutuality, financial risk, part-and-parcel, equipment) and flags residual status risk rather than certifying status.
- Builds a risk register scored by severity and likelihood, with a wording mitigation and a pricing response per risk, and recommends a pricing model (fixed-price per outcome, day-rate capped, or milestone-staged).
- Produces a branded PDF using the founder's own `Context/Brand.md` cues (neutral fallback) plus a markdown working draft.
- UK-focused; IR35 content cites gov.uk and HMRC sources. Not legal or tax advice.
